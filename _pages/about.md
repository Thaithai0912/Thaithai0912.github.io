  <section class="home-section compact-section" id="research">
    <p class="section-kicker">Focus</p>
    <h2 class="section-title">Building language models that are capable, efficient, and useful.</h2>
    <p class="section-lede">
      I care about AI research that survives contact with real problems: models that
      can read long contexts, reason reliably, run efficiently, and support people in
      practical settings. My work spans long-context modeling, efficient architectures,
      pretraining, data engineering, and evaluation.
    </p>

    <div class="research-grid">
      <article class="info-block">
        <h3>Long-context modeling</h3>
        <p>Helping models remember, retrieve, and reason over very long inputs.</p>
      </article>
      <article class="info-block">
        <h3>Efficient architectures</h3>
        <p>Designing sparse, linear, hybrid, and recurrent-style mechanisms for scalable LLMs.</p>
      </article>
      <article class="info-block">
        <h3>Data and evaluation</h3>
        <p>Improving the signals that train models and the benchmarks that reveal their limits.</p>
      </article>
    </div>
